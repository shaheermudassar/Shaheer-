Site Overview

Explore the captivating world of Recommended Videos and Shorts on the Home page. Each Video and Short elegantly presents exclusive information sourced directly from the database, ensuring an immersive experience. Login & Sign up

Experience a seamless Authentication system with a user-friendly touch. Enjoy the convenience of "Login with Google," exclusive for registered testers. Your Channel is automatically created upon sign-up, and you can effortlessly update your information in Youtube Studio.

Youtube Studio

Take full control of your channel with ease through Youtube Studio. Manage, add, or edit your captivating Videos or Shorts effortlessly. Customize your content with the option to set videos as private or public, and tailor the perfect thumbnail for each creation. Stay connected with your audience by updating your channel information seamlessly.

Video Player

Enjoy a fully functional video player similar to YouTube, equipped with exciting features.
