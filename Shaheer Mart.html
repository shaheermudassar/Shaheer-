Site Overview

Explore the main details on the Home page. Each product showcases unique information sourced directly from the database. Real-time reviews provide overall ratings for products and stores. Detailed descriptions accompany each product. Categories have dedicated pages with filtering options for price, tags, and more. Discover vendors on a separate page, with detailed information for each.

Order Products

You can add Products to Your Wishlist and Cart. Buy Products online with Paypal or Cash on delivery. You will get order confirmation on your email and automatically your invoice of the bill will be generated of which you can get the pdf or print.

Login & Sign up

Provides fully functional Authentication system. Makes Your Profile and adds address when new account is created. These informations can be updated in User Dashboard.

User Dashboard

All the stats of orders is here. User can view and Edit his/her information. This includes Profile, Address, Notifications, Orders and order details, Change Password, Logout and Delete Account Features.

Create & Handle Store

You can create your Store and can change information Later and Start selling. It holds Store Dashboard having all revenue and other stats. You can see notifications of orders and much more.

Create & Edit Products

After opening the store, You can Create and Edit Products for your Store and get full info about your products.
